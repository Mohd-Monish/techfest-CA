ABOUT TECHFEST

Born in 1998, its core motto is to promote science and technology and foster genuine interest among students to solve real-life problems.

1,75,000+ Footfall
300+ Events
2500+ National Colleges
500+ International Colleges

Our Vision

To provide an international platform for youth to showcase their technical prowess, witness cutting-edge global technology, and be inspired by world-renowned personalities.

Our Impact

Beyond the main event, Techfest drives year-round Social Initiatives (like STEM literacy, legal awareness, and health drives) and the Ideate platform, connecting innovators with corporations and government bodies for project implementation.
